BADMINTON

Basic Rules
Serial Number Rule
1. A match consists of the best of 3 games of 21 points.
2. At 20 all, the side which gains a 2 point lead first, wins that game.At 29 all, the side scoring the 30th point, wins that game.
3. When the leading score reaches 11 points, players have a 60 second interval.
4. A 2 minute interval between each game is allowed.
5. A player must wait until his opponent is ready before serving. If the opponent attempts a return then he is ruled having been ready.
6. The feet of both players must remain in a stationary position until the serve is made. Your feet can not be touching the line at this time.
7. The shuttle cannot be caught and slung with the racket.
8. A serve or shot that lands outside the court boundaries, passes under or through the net, touches any other obstructions or a players body or clothing. The boundary and service lines are considered in play.
9. A player cannot hold his racket near the net to ward off a downward stroke by his opponent or to interfere with his racket.
10. The shuttle in play is struck before it crosses the net to the striker's side of the net. You may follow through over the net.
